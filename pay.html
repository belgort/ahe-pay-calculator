Part-Time Professor Salary Changes

Part-time faculty and moonlighting faculty can use the calculator below to compute your compensation. For most part-time faculty, your compensation is 72 percent of step A per FTE (parity pay).

Moonlighting full-time faculty and part-time professors for which the parity-pay method does not yet result in a salary increase that exceeds the hourly rate will receive the hourly rate of pay.

Below, you can compute your compensation using each method of payment - parity pay & hourly pay. The higher of the two is how much you'll be compensated.
